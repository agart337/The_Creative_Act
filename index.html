About



The Creative Act: A Way of Being by Rick Rubin is a book about creativity—not just for artists but for everyone.

He believes that creativity isn’t about talent or hard rules; it’s about being open to the world, noticing small details, and letting ideas flow naturally. He encourages people to experiment, make mistakes, and not worry too much about perfection.

Rubin discusses the phases of a creative project, which he calls the Seed phase, the Experiment phase, the Craft phase, and the Completion phase.

This website categories some of my favourite chapters into what I think are part of each phase.
